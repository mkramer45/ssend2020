About SurfSend


  • SurfSend is a web app that provides New England surfers with accurate data regarding current surf conditions & forecasts for the week ahead.

  • Registered users can opt to receive notifications via SMS or Email when our system determines that the conditions will make for a quality day of surfing, all factors considered.

  • SurfSend data is sourced by automated data-mining scripts that pull from reliable resources on the web pertaining to information such as government buoy height/intervals, wind speed/direction, air temp & more.

  • For all questions/feedback, please email surfsendcontact@gmail.com. For development & API inquiries, please email surfsendtech@gmail.com.
